About me

I have one semester left until I complete my BS in Computer Science at Portland State University. I am crazy about user interface and user experience design. I love diving into the tiny details, to make programs that are simple and enjoyable to use.

In addition to school, I work full time at a labor management company, where I do a little bit of everything, from writing an online employee scheduling and turnover database, to producing and filming employee training videos.

When I’m not at work or school, I’m usually at home relaxing, or running around the outdoors somewhere with my wife and dog.
